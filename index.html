Research Initiative

The Revelation Project

H. Cumaili Lab is an independent Abrahamic research initiative dedicated to the study of divine revelation, prophetic continuity, sacred history, and the structure of scripture across the Abrahamic traditions.

The project examines the movement of revelation through history from the Torah and prophetic literature to the Gospel traditions and the Qur’anic revelation exploring the relationship between sacred language, theology, interpretation, and human understanding.

Through comparative textual analysis, semantic research, manuscript studies, hermeneutics, and philosophical inquiry, the research seeks to construct a unified framework for understanding revelation, prophecy, and theological development within the Abrahamic world.

Independent Researcher

H. Cumaili

H. Cumaili is an independent researcher specializing in Biblical Hermeneutics, Hebrew Semantics, Comparative Abrahamic Studies, Prophetic Continuity, and the philosophy of divine revelation.

His work focuses on the continuity of revelation across the Abrahamic scriptures, with particular interest in prophetic transmission, sacred language, manuscript traditions, theological structures, and the relationship between text, interpretation, and sacred history.

Current projects include God or Religion?, The Definition of God, The Revelation Project, and an expanding comparative archive dedicated to scripture, prophecy, theology, and Abrahamic continuity.
