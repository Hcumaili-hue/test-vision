Research Initiative

The Revelation Project

A long-term scholarly initiative dedicated to the study of revelation, prophetic continuity, sacred language, textual development, and theological interpretation across the Abrahamic traditions.

Through comparative analysis, hermeneutics, semantic investigation, manuscript traditions, and philosophical inquiry, the project seeks to explore the continuity of divine revelation and the historical movement of sacred meaning.

Independent researcher specializing in Biblical Hermeneutics, Hebrew Semantics, Comparative Abrahamic Studies, Prophetic Continuity, and the philosophy of divine revelation.
