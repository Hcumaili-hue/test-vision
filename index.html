Functional Research Program

GOD FIRST, THEN RELIGION

God First, Then Religion is a long term scholarly research framework dedicated to the study of divine revelation, prophetic continuity, sacred language, Biblical and Qur’anic hermeneutics, Hebrew semantics, textual criticism, and comparative Abrahamic interpretation.

Through historical, semantic, and comparative analysis, the initiative explores the continuity of revelation, the development of religious meaning, and the relationship between divine reality and religious interpretation across the Abrahamic traditions.
